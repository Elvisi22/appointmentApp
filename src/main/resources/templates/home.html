HealTech Appointment Integration

ON the healthcare system

Purpose

This cutting-edge digital platform has been meticulously designed to enhance efficiency, convenience, and overall patient experience, making it a game-changer in the field of healthcare. With HealTech App , saying goodbye to long waiting times and cumbersome paperwork is just the beginning. Seamlessly integrating state-of-the-art technology, this platform empowers individuals to take control of their vaccination journey like never before.

Why is it needed

Offers a user-friendly and secure solution that simplifies the vaccination appointment booking process, saves time, enhances accessibility, and contributes to a well-organized and efficient healthcare system.

Technologies Used

To develop this kind of application , I have used SpringBoot as the frameWork , including Spring Security for the authorization of access to different users, Spring Web , MySQL as the database and Thymeleaf to design the pages
